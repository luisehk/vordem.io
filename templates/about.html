Our work process

1/4

Discover & Conceptualize

Using different tools like the Design Sprint, User Experience Design and Product Management, this is where we focus on ensuring the software solves a real problem in a way that is helpful to your users and your business.


Outcomes / Deliverables:

  • Product brief
  • Customer journey map
  • Low-fidelity wireframes
  • Real-looking prototype
  • Findings from the user testing
  • Report with next steps

2/4

Plan & Design

Once we have enough certainty about the product, we polish the interface design, write detailed user stories, prioritize features, do data modeling, etc. The goal is to know whats needed to build the product and give the development team what is needed to do so.


Outcomes / Deliverables:

  • Detailed user stories
  • Acceptance criteria
  • Revised wireframes
  • System architecture
  • Database design
  • Finished UI design

3/4

Build & Launch

This is where we focus on the actual development of the product, applying industry best practices such as test-driven development, continuous integration / development, short iterations, code review, among others.


Outcomes / Deliverables:

  • Shipped funcionality every two weeks
  • Weekly project status
  • Stable & maintainable codebase
  • User manual
  • Full source code

4/4

Iterate & Scale

When your digital product is released you need to keep making improvements to it: optimizations, new features, improvements to existing features, automate backups, etc. That's why after shipping your product we continue to work together on a retainer fee, so you can focus on growing your business.


Outcomes / Deliverables:

  • New releases every two weeks
  • Product management
  • Automated backups
  • Application performance monitoring
